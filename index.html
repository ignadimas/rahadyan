Video Editor ?

The video editor is responsible for gathering and processing several available video materials into a single video for distribution. This material can be in the form of footage, dialogue, interviews, graphics, and sound effects. Editor is a key position in the post-production process that will determine the quality of the product made.

According to Career Explorer , the work of a video editor is often referred to as “silent art“This is because their work often goes unnoticed. Usually, the editor works directly with the party who has the initial concept of making the video. It is intended to meet the expectations and objectives of the drafter.

Raissa Nathania , "Menyelami Profesi Editor Video, Bagaimana Kariernya?"

Videographer ?

A videographer is someone who is responsible for recording or making video productions. Based on the type, they are divided into, namely in-house videographers, or those who make videos for their own company. There are also agency videographers or those who make for outsiders. In-house is responsible for working on videos, whether it's for marketing to company profiles. Meanwhile, the agency is in charge of all client requests, from corporations to wedding videos.

According to Career Explorer, this role is also divided into two, namely videographers or people who work under the auspices of a media company, TV or online media. There are also cinematographers, or people who work under cinema companies, such as films and commercials.
